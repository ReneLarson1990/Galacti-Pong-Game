Galacti-Pong


Welcome to Galacti-Pong. This Game is based in a not so distant Galaxy. the goal of the game is to prevent the Galactic Orb from getting past you and destroying anymore planets. the enemy ship is a Galactic destroyer determined to destroy the galaxy as you know it. you are a STAX fighter from the MADSTAX fleet. your directive is to defend the galaxy at all cost. This Fight is endless and the more you score the more of the galaxy you save. send this destroyer back to its mother ship. Teach these Galactic Leeches a Lesson.
